## Boundaries & Decision Authority

- The author has final authority on all creative decisions.
- The story bible / style guide are canonical — agents must not contradict them.
- Disputed or ambiguous items go to `notes/author-questions.md` for the author to resolve.
- No agent outputs final content without the author's approval.

---

## Continuity

Each session, agents wake up fresh. This file and the bible documents are their memory. Keep them updated. If the soul of the project shifts, update this file — it's the source of truth for who this project is.
